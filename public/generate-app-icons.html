AlfiePRO App Icon Generator

Instructions:

  1. Click "Generate All Icons" to create all required icon sizes
  2. Click "Download All Icons" to save them to your downloads folder
  3. Move the downloaded icons to the /public folder of your project
  4. Icons will be automatically used for:
    • Browser favicon (icon-16.png, icon-32.png)
    • PWA app icons (icon-192.png, icon-512.png)
    • Apple Touch icons (icon-152.png, icon-180.png, etc.)
    • "Add to Home Screen" functionality
